ABOUT

Our world is facing the threat of this virus. The pandemic has affected most of the human population.

Looking into this, the government decided to send a team of immensely talented doctors across the world to the most affected parts of the world. And you are one of them.

You will be assigned a country where almost 50% of the population is already infected. You are supposed to utilize your skills to solve the questions given, in order to unlock the vaccine injections to cure them. Your target is to cut the percentage of infected people as much possible and save that country from this pandemic.

But this virus is highly contagious and spreads from one person to another through various modes.
